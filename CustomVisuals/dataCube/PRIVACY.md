Privacy Policy for DataCube 3D Power BI Custom Visual

Last Updated: March 5, 2026
1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the DataCube 3D Power BI custom visual (the "Visual") handles data. As an individual developer, I am committed to ensuring that your data remains private and secure.
2. Data Collection and Transmission

    No Data Collection: The Visual does not collect, store, or maintain any personal or business data provided through the Power BI interface.

    No Data Transmission: The Visual does not transmit any data to external servers, APIs, or third-party services. All data processing and 3D rendering are performed locally within the user's Power BI environment (Power BI Desktop or Power BI Service).

    No Cookies/Tracking: The Visual does not use cookies, web beacons, or any other tracking technologies to monitor user behavior.

3. Third-Party Access

Because the Visual does not collect or transmit data, no third parties have access to your data through this Visual. It operates strictly within the security boundaries defined by the Microsoft Power BI platform.
4. Certification Standards

The Visual is designed to comply with Microsoft’s "Certified Power BI Visual" standards, meaning it does not access external resources or services, ensuring your data never leaves the Power BI ecosystem.
5. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the Visual or for legal and regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted at this URL.
